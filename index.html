Supporting Liverpool Families with SEND

LivPaC is the official parent carer forum for Liverpool, representing families of children and young people with special educational needs and disabilities. We're a voluntary group comprised of dedicated parents and carers who understand the challenges you face.

"Alone we can do so little, but together we can do so much."

Explore Our Resources

Who We Are

LivPaC is run by parents for parents. We understand the challenges families face and work to ensure your voice is heard in the development and delivery of SEND services across Liverpool.

Learn More

What We Do

We gather parents' views, influence policy decisions, provide valuable resources, and create supportive connections to help navigate SEND services.

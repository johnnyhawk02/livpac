Supporting Liverpool Families with SEND

LivPaC is the official parent carer forum for Liverpool, representing families of children and young people with special educational needs and disabilities.

Explore Our Resources

Who We Are

LivPaC is run by parents for parents. We understand the challenges families face and work to ensure your voice is heard.

What We Do

We gather parents' views, influence policy decisions, and provide valuable resources to help navigate SEND services.

Get Involved

Join our forum, attend meetings, or participate in our consultations to help shape local SEND services.

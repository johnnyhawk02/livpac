SEND Activities Calendar

Stay updated with upcoming activities and events suitable for children with SEND in Liverpool. This calendar is a resource for finding inclusive and accessible events in your area and staying connected with the LivPaC community.

Coffee and Connect Sessions

Join us for our Coffee and Connect Sessions throughout the year. These informal meet-ups provide opportunities for parents and carers to connect, share experiences, and offer mutual support.

Major Events

Local Offer LIVE Event

Join us for Liverpool's largest gathering for families to connect with providers and organizations supporting children with SEND. This marketplace event brings online services to life, allowing families to speak face-to-face with support providers and learn about new opportunities.

The next event is currently being planned. Our most recent event was held on July 3rd, 2024 at the Liverpool Guild of Students, University of Liverpool.

Stay tuned for announcements about our next Local Offer LIVE Event!

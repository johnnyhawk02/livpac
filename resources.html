SEND Resources

Welcome to the LivPaC Resources page. The resources included here can be used by both parents/carers and professionals. We've gathered information covering a number of different focus areas, all listed in alphabetical order below to make finding what you need easier.

Share a Key Resource!

Liverpool City Council's Culture Liverpool team have compiled an excellent SEND parenting resource page. We encourage you to explore it and share it widely:

  • Culture Liverpool SEND Parenting Page: https://www.cultureliverpool.co.uk/send-parenting/

Please share this link with your networks, add it to your websites and social media platforms, and share it with your teams to help it reach more parents and carers.

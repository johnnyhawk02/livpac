Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you! Whether you have a question about our resources, want to get involved, or need support navigating SEND services in Liverpool, the LivPaC team is here to help.

Our Contact Details

  • Main Address: The Granby Centre, 79 Granby Street, Liverpool, L8 2TU
  • Telephone: +44 7504 544 638 or 0151 727 5271
  • Email: info@LivPaC.org.uk, livpacpcf@gmail.com or livpac@hotmail.co.uk
  • Operating Hours: Monday to Thursday, 9am to 5pm
  • Access Note: No referral is required to access our services, and most activities are free of charge.

Where to Find Us

We operate from several locations across Liverpool:

  • One-to-One Support: Available at Clubmoor Children's Centre on Utting Avenue East every Thursday from 10am to 2:30pm
  • Coffee Mornings: Regular sessions at various community venues including Blessed Sacrament, Liverpool Lighthouse, and more

Join Our Community

LivPaC welcomes new members who are parents or carers of children with SEND. Membership is free and gives you access to:

  • Regular updates about local services and events
  • Opportunities to have your say in service development
  • Connection to a supportive network of other parents and carers
  • Information about training and workshop opportunities
